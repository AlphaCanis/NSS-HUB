Tree Plantation in Sheran

21/09/2019

The NSS Unit of Chitkara University organised “Tree Plantation” under the SBSI programme on Thursday,15th August 2019 in Vyayamshala of nearby village Sheran .Under the Guidance of Dr. Ashok Kumar NSS Program Coordinator, Faculty members and NSS volunteers planted more than 40 trees .The students also placed bricks as barricades and each student took the responsibility to nourish and maintain the allocated plant.Eventually, the ground attained a glorious look. The committee decided to proceed with the same inspiration, enthusiasm and zeal to enhance the beauty of the environment.We should take care of Mother Nature!

Women's Day Health CheckUp Camp

08/03/2020

To celebrate International Women’s Day is annually held on March 8 .Our college celebrated this auspicious day with great enthusiasm and proudness on 8th March 2020.We organise a free women health checkup camp on this day Every student contributed towards its preparations and the celebration began with speech by NSS volunteers on the topic Women health.Women health is a very important topic and it is the right of every women so except that a quiz is also organised on this day.At last a nukkad natak is performed on women rights.  At last, sweets and refreshments were distributed among students.
